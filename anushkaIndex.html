HOD’s Message


Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Mumbai. Since its inception in 2001, our department has been committed to providing a holistic and globally recognized technical education, equipping students with the knowledge and skills to excel in this dynamic field

Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering plays a crucial role in shaping the future of technology, offering diverse career opportunities across various industries. At our department, we foster a culture of innovation and collaboration, empowering students to work in multidisciplinary teams, develop leadership qualities, and uphold ethical engineering practices. Through expert lectures, hands-on workshops, and industry-driven training programs organized under IEEE and IETE student branches, we ensure that our students stay ahead in emerging technological advancements.

Beyond technical excellence, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals by encouraging participation in co-curricular and extracurricular activities. Our alumni have made remarkable contributions both nationally and internationally, bringing pride to our institution. We remain committed to our mission of developing skilled professionals who not only contribute to technological advancements but also serve society with integrity and responsibility—the Don Bosco way.

We look forward to continuing our journey of excellence and innovation, preparing our students for a successful and impactful career in the ever-evolving world of electronics and telecommunications.

Ms. Madhavi Suryakant Pednekar
Head of Department,
Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
